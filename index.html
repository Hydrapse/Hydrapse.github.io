About Me

I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Southern California, supervised by Prof. Viktor Prasanna. Before that, I got my B.S. degree from Central South University, advised by Prof. Ning Gui. My current research focuses on developing and evaluating coding agents with graph-structured reasoning.
